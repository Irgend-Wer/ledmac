The second MWE, for your convenience, is the same, but using explicit \pstart\pend pairs.
 Even with the AtEveryPend, it works cleanly.


 \documentclass{book}
 \usepackage[series={A}]{eledmac}
 \AtEveryPend{\vskip 5ex}

 \begin{document}

 \begingroup
 \beginnumbering
 \numberpstarttrue

 \pstart
 Paragraph 1 goes here.

 \pend

 \pstart
 Paragraph 2 goes here.

 \pend

 \endnumbering
 \endgroup
 \end{document}
